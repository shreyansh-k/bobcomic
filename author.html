Shrey Chakraborty

Born in the bustling streets of Purulia District in Bengal, he really doesn't know whether his liking of Rasgulla is normal, although he craves shawarma alongside it. He's the co-author of Bob, and although Bob doesn't want to admit it, he says that 50% of bob's stories are based on him.

Samvart is a Class 5 creator from Bengaluru who turns everyday problems into funny, fast-paced adventures. He writes cheeky stories, sketches quirky characters, and designs clever game levels that surprise kids and kids-at-heart.
